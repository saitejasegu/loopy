## Promotional text

## Description
Loopy helps you keep small daily loops going.

• Track yes/no, countable, and timed habits
• Optional Apple Health habits for steps and active energy
• Local reminders on the days a habit is due
• Stats, calendar history, and streak-aware analytics
• Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets
• Shortcuts / Siri check-ins via App Intents
• Private iCloud sync between your devices
• Achievements based on real progress

Your check-ins stay as dated history, so editing today never erases the past.
